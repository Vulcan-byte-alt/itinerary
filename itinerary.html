Day-by-Day Itinerary

1

Barcelona, Spain

Embark and explore Gaud's masterpieces

Your luxury cruise begins in the enchanting city of Barcelona. Embark on the magnificent Regent Seven Seas Explorer and settle into your all-suite accommodation.

Highlights:

  • Visit La Sagrada Familia and Park Gell
  • Stroll down Las Ramblas
  • Explore the Gothic Quarter
  • Enjoy tapas and Spanish wine
4

Gibraltar

The Rock and famous Barbary macaques

Discover the iconic Rock of Gibraltar, a British territory at the southern tip of Spain with stunning views of two continents.

Highlights:

  • Cable car to the top of the Rock
  • Meet the famous Barbary macaques
  • Visit St. Michael's Cave
  • Explore the Great Siege Tunnels
5

Madeira, Portugal

Floating garden of the Atlantic

Experience the lush Portuguese island of Madeira, known for its dramatic landscapes, botanical gardens, and world-famous fortified wine.

Highlights:

  • Visit Monte Palace Tropical Garden
  • Ride the famous cable cars
  • Taste Madeira wine at a local winery
  • Explore Funchal's historic center
15

Miami, USA

Arrive in vibrant South Florida

Your unforgettable transatlantic journey concludes in the vibrant city of Miami. Disembark with memories of a lifetime.

Optional Extensions:

  • Explore Art Deco South Beach
  • Visit Wynwood Walls street art
  • Shop on Lincoln Road
  • Extend your stay in Florida

SHIP & CABINS

Explore Ships By Day

Discover the luxurious amenities and elegant spaces aboard your floating palace

Upper Deck Promenade

Our ship features a revolutionary design that maximizes space, comfort, and ocean views. The innovative layout includes multiple outdoor decks, intimate lounges, and spacious public areas that never feel crowded. The grand atrium serves as the heart of the ship, featuring a stunning crystal chandelier and marble staircases.

The Wine Vault

Our beverage program features an exceptional selection of over 500 wines from prestigious vineyards worldwide. Our expert sommeliers have personally selected each bottle to complement our culinary offerings. The Wine Vault houses rare and vintage selections, while our bars offer premium spirits, craft cocktails, and local specialties from ports of call.

Owner's Suite

Every stateroom and suite is designed as a sanctuary of comfort and elegance. Our accommodations feature luxury bedding with Egyptian cotton linens, marble bathrooms with premium toiletries, and thoughtful amenities including interactive TVs, refrigerated mini-bars, and complimentary WiFi. Balcony staterooms offer private outdoor spaces to enjoy ocean views and fresh sea breezes.

Luxury Spa Suites

Our award-winning wellness center offers a sanctuary of tranquility at sea. The spa features treatment rooms offering massages, facials, and body treatments using premium products from renowned brands. Our thermal suite includes aromatic steam rooms, saunas, and hydrotherapy pools with heated ceramic loungers.

Sunset Cocktail Bar

Experience handcrafted cocktails with premium spirits and fresh ingredients. Our expert mixologists create signature drinks inspired by destinations along your journey. Enjoy complimentary top-shelf spirits, craft cocktails, and local specialties from ports of call throughout your cruise.
